OSSOS is a survey of the sky using MegaCam on the 3.8-metre Canada-France-Hawaii Telescope.
We aim to discover a thousand distant icy worlds.

Survey

OSSOS explores the resonant populations of the Kuiper belt, surveying 168 deg2 to magnitude 24.5 in r band. More »

Science

With dedicated tracking of discoveries, OSSOS provides both high-quality orbits & a survey simulator for testing models of the Solar System's evolution. More »
